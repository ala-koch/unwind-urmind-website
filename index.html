About Us

Unwind Urmind was born from the unique bond of friendship and a shared love for music, nurtured by the unspoken magic of afterhours gatherings. Our collective thrives on mutual inspiration, elevating each other's creative energies to craft immersive atmospheres that transcend the ordinary. We are driven by a desire to create not just events, but experiences—spaces where groove, connection, and freedom of expression come alive. On the 17th, we invite you to step into our world. Let’s dance, connect, and celebrate the soundscape we’ve crafted together.
